20 | tattvadaśaka |sadasadyogahīnāyai tathatāyai namo namaḥ |anāvilā yataḥ saiva bodhato bodhirūpiṇī

na sākāranirākāre tathatāṃ jñātumicchataḥ |madhyamā'madhyamā caiva guruvāganalaṅkṛtā

bodhirasau bhaved bhāvaḥ saṅgaṃ tyaktā svabhāvataḥ |āsaṅgo bhrāntito yāto bhrāntirasthānikā
matā

kiṃ tattvaṃ vastuno rūpaṃ rūpaṃ cārūpakaṃ yataḥ |arūpaṃ ca bhaved rūpaṃ phalahetusvabhāvataḥ

evameva rasā dharmmā nirāsaṃṅgā nirāspadāḥ |prabhāsvarā amī sarvve yathābhūtasamādhinā

yathābhūtasamādhiśca bhavet prasthānacittataḥ |ajasraṃ jāyate tattvaṃ yasmāt tat padavedinām

jñānajñeyavihīnaṃ [tu]jagadevādvayaṃ matam |dvayahīnābhiropaśca tathaiva hi prabhāsvaraḥ

etat tattvāvarodhena yena tena yathā tathā |vivṛtākṣo bhramed yogī keśarīva samantataḥ

lokadharmmavyatīto'sau unmattavratamāśritaḥ |sārdhaṃ karotyātālambaḥ svādhiṣṭānavibhūṣitaḥ

--------------------------------------------------------samāsamamatā hitvā jñātumahānāyādhanāḥ

||tattvadaśakaḥ samāptaḥ |kṛtiriyaṃ paṇḍitāvadhūtādvayavajrapādānāmiti ||
